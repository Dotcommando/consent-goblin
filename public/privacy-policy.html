Goblin Consent

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Goblin Consent is a Chrome extension for creating consent dialog mockups for screenshots, demos, design reviews, and visual mockups.

What data the extension handles

The extension stores the dialog content you create, such as:

  • site label text
  • dialog title text
  • dialog list items
  • button labels
  • selected presets and edited values

How data is stored

This data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. It is used only to keep your current mock dialog state available between the editor panel and the on-page preview.

What the extension does not do

  • It does not send your dialog content to the developer.
  • It does not transmit your data to external servers.
  • It does not collect account credentials, payment details, or personal identifiers.
  • It does not track your browsing history beyond the active tab interaction needed to display the mock dialog on the page you choose.
  • It does not sell or share your data with third parties.

Permissions

Goblin Consent uses the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab and scripting to insert the mock consent dialog into the current page after you explicitly click the extension.
  • sidePanel to open the editor UI in Chrome’s side panel.
  • storage to save your dialog configuration locally in the browser.

Data sharing

Goblin Consent does not share stored dialog data with the developer, service providers, advertisers, analytics platforms, or other third parties.

Data retention and control

Your saved dialog data remains in your local browser storage until you overwrite it, reset it, clear the extension’s storage, or remove the extension.
